This section will hold FREE's recurring-use templates — the working tools chapters
reach for again and again.

The **T-series** templates are in development and will be added here as they are
finalized. Planned templates include:

- **Meeting agendas** for General Assemblies and Circle meetings
- **Synopsis formats** for recording what an assembly decided
- **Sign-up sheets** for events and working groups
- Other day-to-day organizing tools

Until these are published, chapters can adapt the practices described in the
[Starter Kit](/starter-kit/f1-what-is-a-free-chapter/) and in Tulsa's
[chapter materials](/chapters/tulsa/).
